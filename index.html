About Me

Gabriel de Diego

Virtual Reality Developer

My passion stems from a childhood vision to interact with computers beyond a 2D screen. Up until a handful of years ago, the potential of Immersive Computing remained limited within science fiction worlds. Today, we are witnessing the emmergence of what many are calling the "final computing platform". Both augmented and virtual reality will have a profound impact in the way we do business, interact with computers, and ultimately how we interact with one another. I plan to be at the forefront of this new paradigm shift.
